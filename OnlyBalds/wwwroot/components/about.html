Our Purpose

We Embrace Baldness

Only Balds is a community-driven brand created to support and empower anyone navigating the journey of hair loss. Whether you're bald, balding, considering shaving, or exploring options like hair transplants, we’re here to provide honest guidance, high-quality products, and a space where you feel seen and understood. Our mission is to help you take care of yourself, build confidence, and connect with others who truly get it — because no matter where you are in your journey, you’re not alone.

Our Story

From John

Only Balds started from my own journey of losing my hair and eventually embracing the decision to shave it all off. What I realized through that process is that there weren’t many places that truly supported people through the different stages of hair loss — whether you're holding on, thinking about shaving, exploring hair transplants, or fully bald and proud. Only Balds was created to be that space: a supportive, honest, and welcoming community for anyone navigating their hair journey. Our goal is to offer real resources, high-quality products, and a sense of belonging no matter where you're at. We're here to remind you that you're not alone, and there’s strength, confidence, and connection waiting on the other side.
